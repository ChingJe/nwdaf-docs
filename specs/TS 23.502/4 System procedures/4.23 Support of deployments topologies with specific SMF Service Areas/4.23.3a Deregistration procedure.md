# 4.23.3a Deregistration procedure

The following impacts are applicable to clause 4.2.2.3 (Deregistration procedures) when the UE has established PDU Session(s):

The V-SMF is replaced with I-SMF and the H-SMF is replaced with SMF.

In step 2, if ULCL/BP is included in the data path, the I-SMF releases the local UPF (PSA) and includes N4 information in the Nsmf_PDUSession_Release request.
