# 4.11.0a.2a.1a SM Policy Association Establishment in EPS

The following enhancement is applicable to clause 4.11.0a.2 which refers to clause 4.16.4 (SM Policy Association Establishment):

\- Step 1 of clause 4.16.4: If the SMF+PGW-C receives URSP Provisioning Support indication, the SMF+PGW-C selects the PCF based on the URSP delivery in EPS capability of the PCF, as defined in clause 6.3.7.1 of TS 23.501 \[2\].
