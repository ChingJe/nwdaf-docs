# 4.15.6.3b 5G VN group data

The 5G VN group data is described in Table 4.15.6.3b-1.

Table 4.15.6.3b-1: Description of 5G VN group data

<table>
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 38%" />
<col style="width: 61%" />
</colgroup>
<thead>
<tr class="header">
<th>Parameters</th>
<th>Description</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr class="odd">
<td>DNN</td>
<td>DNN for the 5G VN group.</td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td>S-NSSAI</td>
<td>S-NSSAI for the 5G VN group.</td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td>PDU Session Type</td>
<td>PDU Session Types allowed for 5G VN group.</td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td>Application descriptor</td>
<td>There may be multiple instances of this information; this information may be used to build URSP sent to 5G VN group members (NOTE 1).</td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td>Information related with secondary authentication / authorization</td>
<td><p>This may indicate:</p>
<p>- the need for secondary authentication/authorization (as defined in clause 5.6 of TS 23.501 [2]);</p>
<p>- the need for SMF to request the UE IP address from the DN-AAA Server.</p>
<p>If at least one of secondary authentication/authorization or DN-AAA UE IP address allocation is needed, the AF may provide DN-AAA Server addressing information.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td>5G VN group communication indication</td>
<td>Indicates that the 5G VN group is associated with 5G VN group communication.</td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td>Maximum Group Data Rate</td>
<td><p>This limits the total bit rate that is expected to be provided across all sessions of a 5G VN group.</p>
<p>[optional].</p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td colspan="2">NOTE 1: As described in TS 23.503 [20], the PCF may be configured with a mapping from Application Descriptor to other information required to construct the URSP rules, e.g. IP filters and SSC mode.</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

The information described in Table 4.15.6.3b-1 corresponds to 5G VN group data that an AF may provide together with External Group ID.
