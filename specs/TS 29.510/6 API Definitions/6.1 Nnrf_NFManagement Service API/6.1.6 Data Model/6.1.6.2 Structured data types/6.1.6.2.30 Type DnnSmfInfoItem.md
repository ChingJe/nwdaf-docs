<td colspan="5"><p>NOTE 1: For a SMF which only supports the I-SMF related functionalities, the dnn attribute may be an invalid DNN according to operator's local policy.</p>
<p>NOTE 2: The Wildcard DNAI included in the "dnaiList" attribute indicates that the SMF can be selected for this DNN for any DNAI. The absence of "dnaiList" attribute does not mean that the SMF (e.g. pre-Rel-17 compliant) does not support any DNAI, but the SMF did not indicate which DNAIs it may support.</p>
<p>NOTE 3: The absence of this IE means that the SMF supports LBO PDU sessions for inbound roaming UEs from any PLMN. For inbound roaming UEs from PLMNs which have no dedicated SMF configured for establishing LBO PDU sessions, the SMF whose NF profile does not include the uePlmnRangeList shall be selected. An SMF with the uePlmnRangeList confgiured may still be selected for non-roaming UEs.</p></td>
