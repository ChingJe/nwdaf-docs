<tr class="even">
<td>reqQosMonParams</td>
<td>array(RequestedQosMonitoringParameter)</td>
<td>1..N</td>
<td><p>Indicates the QoS information to be measured, e.g.UL packet delay, DL packet delay and/or round trip packet delay between the UE and the UPF is to be monitored when the QoS Monitoring for packet delay is enabled for the service data flow.</p>
<p>If the "EnQoSMon" feature is supported, the indication of QoS monitoring for congestion (e.g., the UL and/or the DL congestion indication) or data rate (e.g., the UL and/or the DL data rate indication) may also be provided.</p>
<p>If the "EnQoSMon" feature is supported and the "PACK_DELAY_VAR" event is subscribed, it indicates whether PDV measurement is for the UL, DL and/or round trip packet delay.</p>
<p>If the "EnQoSMon" feature is supported and the "RT_DELAY_TWO_QOS_FLOWS" event is subscribed, it indicates round trip packet delay.</p>
<p>If the "ListUE_5G" feature is supported, the indication of QoS monitoring for consolidated data rate (e.g., the UL and/or the DL data rate indication) may also be provided.</p></td>
<td></td>
</tr>

<td></td>

<td>repThreshDl</td>
<td>Uinteger</td>
<td>0..1</td>
<td><p>Unsigned integer identifying a threshold in units of milliseconds for DL packet delay measurement reports.</p>
<p>If the "EnQoSMon" feature is supported and the "PACK_DELAY_VAR" event is subscribed, it indicates the threshold for DL PDV measurement reports.</p>
<p>It shall be present when the "reqQosMonParams" attribute includes "DOWNLINK".</p></td>

<p>It shall be present when the "reqQosMonParams" attribute includes "UPLINK".</p></td>
<td></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td>repThreshRp</td>
<td>Uinteger</td>
<td>0..1</td>
<td><p>Unsigned integer identifying a threshold in units of milliseconds for round trip packet delay measurement reports.</p>
<p>If the "EnQoSMon" feature is supported and the "PACK_DELAY_VAR" event is subscribed, it indicates the threshold for round trip PDV measurement reports.</p>
<p>If the "EnQoSMon" feature is supported and the "RT_DELAY_TWO_QOS_FLOWS" event is subscribed, it indicates the threshold for round trip delay for two QoS flows (i.e. the UL traffic and DL traffic of the service data flow are separated into two QoS flows respectively) measurement reports.</p></td>
<td></td>

<td>EnQoSMon, GMEC</td>

<td>EnQoSMon, GMEC</td>

It shall be present when the requested QoS monitoring parameter is packet delay and when the "repFreqs" attribute includes "PERIODIC" or "EVENT_TRIGGERED".</p></td>
